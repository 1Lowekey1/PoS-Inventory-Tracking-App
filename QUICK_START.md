# ⚡ QUICK START GUIDE (v2.0)

Get your booth up and running in minutes with Booth POS v2.0.

## 🎯 3 Steps to Your First Sale

### Step 1: Add Your Ingredients
1. Open the **Inventory** tab.
2. Tap **+ New Ingredient**.
3. Add your main supplies (e.g., "Coffee Beans", "Milk", "16oz Cups").
4. Enter the total quantity you have on hand.

### Step 2: Create Your Products
1. Open the **Products** tab.
2. Tap **+ New Product**.
3. Set your **Selling Price**.
4. **Build the Recipe**: Choose which ingredients are used and how much (e.g., "18g Coffee", "150ml Milk", "1 piece Cup").
5. Save the product.

### Step 3: Start the Event & Sell!
1. Go to the **Cashier** tab.
2. Tap **Start Event**.
3. Name your event and enter your **Total Fixed Cost** (how much you spent on supplies).
4. **Make a Sale**: Tap a product, choose the quantity, and confirm!

## 💡 Pro Tips for v2.0

### 🧪 Use Demo Mode First
Before your event starts, go to **Settings** and toggle **Demo Mode**. This allows you to practice making sales without actually deducting from your real inventory. When you're ready for the real thing, turn it off and start your event!

### 📊 Understanding Profit
Your profit in v2.0 is calculated as:
**Revenue** (what you sold) minus **Fixed Cost** (what you spent).
The goal is to reach the "Break-even" point as quickly as possible.

### 📦 Quick Inventory Adjustments
In the **Inventory** tab, use the **Add Stock** and **Remove** buttons on each card to quickly update your counts without opening a full edit window.

### 🌓 Dark Mode
Tap the **Settings** tab to switch to Dark Mode. It's easier on the eyes during late-night events and can help save battery on mobile devices.

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

- **"No Active Event" error?** You must tap "Start Event" before you can record real sales.
- **Product button disabled?** You are likely "Out of Stock" for one of the ingredients in that product's recipe. Restock in the Inventory tab.
- **Data missing?** Ensure you are using the same browser and device. If you cleared your browser cache, data might be lost unless you have a backup.
